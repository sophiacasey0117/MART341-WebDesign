About Me


Hello! I'm Sophia Casey. I was born, raised, and still currently live in Missoula, Montana. I've been writing ever since I was little, from full length novels to short stories and poetry. I started my screenwriting journey in my first year of college since I'm a Media Arts/Film major and it's been something I've continued throughout my college career.

Throughout the years I've written a good amount of screenplays for short films, as well as the pilot of a TV series I came up with for a Writing for TV class. I've posted my screenplays here in the 'Scripts' page. Feel free to take a look for any inspiration or examples.
