Why I Chose These Website Ideas

1. Portfolio

I want to create a photography portfolio so I have something to show clients or employers what I have done in the future when I'm trying to get hired and into a more professional work environment. I also think it would be a nice place to keep all of my work. It would be more organized AND stylized.

I hope to find out more about myself, my personal style/branding and what I represent through this process of trying to create my own portfolio.

2. Personal Blog

I think a personal blog would be interesting to develop and work on as a way to track my experiences, my health (mental and physical), as well as to keep a digital trail of my life. In this personal blog, I would treat it kind of like a bullet journal. I would track my moods, do diary entries, and store memories or moments from that day that stuck out to me.

I see using this personal blog as a way to stay in touch with myself and MY needs. A way to keep myself on track and stay organized in my head.

3. Travel Journal

A travel journal would be a fun way to collect memories and save my thoughts on the places I went and experiences I had. I think it would be really cool to have this digital journal appear like a real one. Where you can bookmark different placea I would travel, flip between pages, and for each section it would be a continuous vertical flow of memories.

I currently keep a physical travel/adventure book that I have been printing pictures into and writing little notes about it. I think it would be awesome to be able to just upload pictures to the website and not have to spend a lot on film. Plus, I would always move things around on the website or change up the feel of it, whereas with a physical book I'm more limited to what I can modify.
