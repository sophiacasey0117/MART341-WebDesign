Hello, I'm Sophia Casey!

I’m a Missoula, MT based photographer who strives to capture the inner beauty of the world and the people who inhabit it. Whether that be in a candid nature or inside of a controlled studio environment, my goal is to photograph the simple yet beautiful moments of day to day life.

In my work, I judge, discriminate, hate || encourage, empower, and explore.
