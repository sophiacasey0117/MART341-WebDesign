About Me

Sophia Casey is a Missoula, MT based photographer who strives to capture the inner beauty of the world and the people who inhabit it. Whether that be in a candid nature or inside of a controlled studio environment, her goal is to photograph the simple yet beautiful moments of day to day life.

In Sophia's work, she judges, discriminates, hates || encourages, empowers, and explores.
